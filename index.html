I'm a postdoctoral researcher at Gulbenkian Institute in Portugal where I work on molecular evolution and method/software development for analyzing biological data.
In my spare time I do landscape photography, tinker with electronics, and write code to interact with the physical world.

I hold a PhD degree in Bioinformatics from Gulbenkian Institute (Portugal), and an MSc in Biotechnology from Jagiellonian University (Poland).
